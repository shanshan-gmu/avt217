The Blakery

https://www.theblakery.co/

Intended Audience

The Blakery's intended audience seeks anyone who is interested in a swwet treat made for any occasion whether it is a birthday, work event, or just a random day, they have you covered! For the specific age group, I would say primarily young adults or even teens since the website has a lot of different graphics and images of all of the products that they are selling, which definitely appeals to this audience.

Primary Actions

Some of the primary actions of this website include: browsing the 18 different flavors and buying cookies either individually or in a pack, learn more about the founder, signing up for their newsletter, etc. but their overall primary action is getting you to buy cookies with a fun and engaging website that showcases the products well and entices you to buy their products.
